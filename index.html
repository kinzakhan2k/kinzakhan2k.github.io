Projects

The Third Place Community Plaza

In suburban areas of Brampton, residents often feel disconnected from their neighbors, leading to a lack of social interaction and support networks. This project seeks to bridge this gap by creating a vibrant community plaza that transcends zoning barriers, fostering connections and collaboration among neighbors.

Revitalization of the Lansdowne Underpass

Transforming the Lansdowne Underpass into a vibrant and safe community space, the project combines environmental consciousness with urban design innovation. From enhancing graffiti artwork with LED lighting to installing a rain collection system and improving accessibility with a ramp-equipped staircase, this project reimagines underpass infrastructure.
